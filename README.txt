society

> this little program "emulates" a society.

* every 2 seconds a person has a 50%/50% chance to be added
* every second, a person with less than 20 lifetime has a 50%/50% chance to gain 50 money or lose between 0 and 30 money
* every second, a person with/over 20 lifetime has a 50%/50% chance to gain between 175 and 715 money or lose between 10 and 300 money
* if a person has over 80 lifetime, that person has a 10% chance to get removed, if someone does get removed, nobody else will get removed that day
